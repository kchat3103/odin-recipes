Shahi Paneer

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of pasta, meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is a hearty and flavorful dish that is loved for its comforting qualities and delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Cashews
  • Fresh cream
  • Ghee or oil
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chili
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

Recipe instructions

  1. Saut√© onions, green chili, and ginger-garlic paste in ghee or oil.
  2. Add tomatoes and cashews, then cook until soft.
  3. Blend the mixture into a smooth paste.
  4. Heat ghee or oil in a pan, add the spice powders and cook briefly.
  5. Pour in the tomato-cashew paste and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add fresh cream, salt, sugar, and paneer cubes. Cook for a few minutes.
  7. Crush and sprinkle kasuri methi, and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.
