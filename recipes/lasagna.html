Lasagna

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that consists of multiple layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, alternating with various fillings such as sauce, cheese, and meat or vegetables.

The layers are then baked until the pasta is tender, and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna is known for its rich and hearty flavors, making it a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef or sausage (for a meaty version) or sautéed vegetables (for a vegetarian version)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or puree
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Dried herbs (such as oregano and basil)
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe instructions

  1. Cook lasagna pasta sheets until al dente, then drain.
  2. Prepare tomato sauce with onions, garlic, and tomato puree.
  3. Cook ground beef (or sauté vegetables) with onions, garlic, and seasonings.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, Parmesan, eggs, and herbs.
  5. Layer pasta, tomato sauce, filling, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and top with tomato sauce and shredded mozzarella.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes.
  8. Let it rest, then garnish with fresh herbs before serving.
