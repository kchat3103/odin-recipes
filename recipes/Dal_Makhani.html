Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is a popular Indian dish made from black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked with aromatic spices and finished with a touch of cream. It is known for its rich and creamy texture, making it a comforting and flavorful choice.

Ingredients

  • Black lentils (urad dal)
  • Kidney beans (rajma)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Ghee or oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Spices (such as turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala)
  • Butter
  • Cream

Recipe instructions

  1. Soak black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma) overnight, then cook them until tender.
  2. Saut√© onions, garlic, and cumin seeds in ghee or oil.
  3. Add tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Add the cooked lentils and beans to the tomato mixture, along with water. Simmer for a few hours.
  5. Stir in butter and cream. Adjust salt and spices to taste.
  6. Garnish with kasuri methi and fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with rice, naan, or roti.
